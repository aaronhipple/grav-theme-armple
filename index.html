How We Work

Interested in having us build a website for you and do marketing, but concerned about what that process will look like? Not to worry! We want you to know how we work up front so you’ll have a better idea if we’re a good fit for you.

Website Design & Build Process

Understanding Your Needs

Before we even quote a website or marketing services, we want to understand what your needs are. Do you need to update an existing website, or are you building from scratch? What are the things your website will need to do? Who are your customers? When people go to your website, what do you want them to do? What are your main goods or services that you want to promote on your website? We have lots of questions. We want to get to know you and understand what you need. Determine the general layout of the site, including the pages Talk about content needs--what does the client have and what needs to be written or created? Create a rough framework that considers the site map and the general layout and structure Discuss features with regard to how consumers will interact with your website, what their experience will be like

Scope of Work and Quote

Based on what we learn about you and your needs for a website, we’ll develop a quote. It’s more than just a price on a piece of paper, though. We’ll spell out the scope of work, telling you about all the things that we’re going to do in order to achieve the things you told us were important for your website. We will provide costs for each part of the scope. We believe in having transparency with our clients—you know what we’re going to do, we do it, and we have an agreed-upon rate for being paid. Generally, we do fixed fee contracts so our clients know costs paying up-front.
