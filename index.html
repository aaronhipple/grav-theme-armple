About Us

Two nerds who grew up to be cool kids!

Aaron Hipple

Aaron is a full stack developer who likes to learn new technical things every day. He’s always on the lookout for a new way to code more efficiently, a new platform, or a piece of technology that makes building websites a smoother, more successful process. He’s also an accomplished jazz guitarist, a talented amateur photographer, and loves the outdoors. He’s lived in Colorado for nearly nine years, and enjoys downhill and Nordic skiing in the winter, and hiking, biking, camping, and paddle-boarding in the summer. He has a legitimate interest in understanding the finer points of investing and setting up 401k plans. Aaron recently moved to Carbondale, CO, where he lives with Colette.

Colette Armstrong

Colette is the project manager and creative who likes to work with clients to create beautiful, functional websites as well as come up with creative marketing solutions to help small businesses reach existing and new customers. She has a background in marketing, technical and business writing and writing, and graphic design and layout. She is also a part-time seamstress, crafting unique projects, as well as providing alterations and repairs for clothing. She likes to draw, sew, knit, paint, and read. In the summer, she likes to hike, bike, and paddle-board; in the winter, snowshoeing and Nordic skiing are her go-to outside activities. Colette grew up in Carbondale, CO, and after a few years living elsewhere for college and jobs, she’s back in the Valley, living in Carbondale, CO, with her cat and Aaron.
